4. Selection.

Sponsor will select the number of Entrants that is specified in the Campaign who received the highest number of Entries during the Contest Period as the winners (“Winners”), in Sponsor’s sole judgment. Winners will be notified by email within five (5) days of the end of the Contest Period. The odds of winning depend on how many entries are made and your relative skill in making numerous entries. To claim a reward, a Winner should follow the instructions contained in his or her notification. Sponsor’s decisions shall be final and binding on all matters relating to the Contest selections.

5. Rewards and Approximate Retail Value (“ARV”).

The reward is what is described either in the Campaign, including the contribution flow, for referring others to the Contest (the “Reward”). The Reward will be awarded to each of the Winners. The ARV is as described in the Campaign. Total ARV of Rewards is the number of Winners multiplied by the ARV of a Reward. The Total ARV of Rewards cannot exceed $5,000. In the event the Total ARV of Rewards exceeds $5,000, the Sponsor will reduce the number of Winners, such that the Total ARV of Rewards will be less than $5,000. The new Winners will then be those Entrants that received the highest number of Entries during the Contest Period, such that the total ARV of Rewards for the Winners is less than $5,000. Odds of winning depend on the number of eligible entries received.
