Tilt Referral Program Terms

The Tilt Referral Program allows registered Tilt users to earn a Referral Reward by referring friends to use Tilt and for those referred friends to receive money.

The referral program is subject to some important rules and limitations that vary by country, so please read these terms carefully. By participating in the program you agree to these terms, which become part of the applicable Tilt Terms of Use.

Definitions

A Available Countries means the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Ireland, Sweden, Norway, and Denmark.

A Tilt User is a user that has an active account on Tilt at the time of the referral.

A Referral SMS is an SMS sent via the Tilt User's phone number from the native SMS composer made availale from within the Tilt mobile application, or an SMS sent on behalf of the Tilt User from Tilt's short-code 818-62.

A Referral Link is a non-user-specific URL that is shared via SMS.

A Referred Friend is someone who is not a Tilt User that receives a Referral Link from a Tilt User through the Services.

Terms that are not defined here will have the same meaning given to them in the version of the applicable Tilt Terms of Use.

How to Earn Money

Under this Referral Program, Tilt Users with accounts denoted as one of the Available Countries can earn a Referral Reward by sending a Referral SMS to a Referred Friend, and having that Referred Friend (i) create a Tilt account on the iOS or Android mobile application and (ii) a verify their phone number via the two-step mechanism provided during account creation. Only the first Tilt User to send a Referral SMS to Referred Friend will be eligible to earn a Reward.

Referred Friends can earn a Referral Reward by (i) creating a Tilt account denoted as one of the Available Countries on the iOS or Android mobile application and (ii) a verifying their phone number via the two-step mechanism provided during account creation.

Once the criteria above has been met, the Tilt User and Referred Friend are eligible to receive for the following referral bonus:

Payment

Any money earned under this Referral Program is sent to both the Tilt User and Referred Friend via one of Tilt's profile's using the Pay feature. Funds will be deposited to their bank account on file.

Referral SMS

You can send a Referral SMS only for personal and non-commercial purposes. Please share the Referral SMS with friends who will appreciate being invited to Tilt. Don’t share Referral SMSes with people you don’t know personally.

Multiple Referrals

A Tilt User referring a Referred Friend will only earn a Referral Reward if the Referred Friend creates a Tilt account for the first time with Tilt and the timestamp reflects a date and time occuring after the timestamp of the Referral SMS.

Status of Past Invites

Tilt users can check on the status of past invitations by emailing happiness@tilt.com.

Limitations

No Referral Reward can be earned through the referral program as a result of fraudulent or illegal activities, or any other conduct that violates our terms of use or other policies. Neither Tilt Users nor Referred Friends can earn a Referral Reward through the Referral Program by creating multiple Tilt profiles.

Tilt reserves the right to investigate and review all activities related to the Referral Program and can cancel or modify referrals for any reason. If Tilt notices any activity that we believe is abusive, fraudulent, illegal, or violative of our Terms of Use or other policies, we may suspend profiles or restrict access to the referral program.
