Tilt Referral Program Terms

The Tilt Referral Program allows registered Tilt users to earn a Referral Reward by referring friends to use Tilt and for those referred friends to receive money towards their Tilt page.

The referral program is subject to some important rules and limitations that vary by country, so please read these terms carefully. By participating in the program you agree to these terms, which become part of the applicable Tilt Terms of Use.

Definitions

A Tilt User is a user that has an active account on Tilt at the time of the referral.

A Referral Link is a unique URL assigned to a Tilt User that enables that user to track and earn rewards for their referrals.

A Referred Friend is someone who is not a Tilt User that receives a Referral Link from a Tilt User through the Services.

Select Countries means The Netherlands, France, Denmark, Finland, Iceland, Norway, and Sweden.

Terms that are not defined here will have the same meaning given to them in the version of the applicable Tilt Terms of Use.

How to Earn Money

Under this Referral Program, Tilt Users can earn a Referral Reward by having Referred Friend creating (i) a Tilt account using the Tilt User’s Referral Link and (ii) a Qualifying Tilt Page. Only the first Tilt User to send a Referral Link to Referred Friend will be eligible to earn a Reward.

Referred Friends can earn a Referral Reward by creating a Qualifying Tilt Page.

Qualifying Tilt Pages

A Qualifying Tilt Page is a Tilt page that is created by a Referred Friend that:

  • is created within 90 calendar days of the Referred Friend creating a Tilt account using the Referral Link;
  • has at least three or more unique contributors who aren’t the Referred Friend or the Tilt User;
  • achieves the tilt amount, which is the minimum funding goal for a Tilt page;
  • is successfully paid out to the Referred Friend; and
  • achieves the minimum tilt value of the Tilt’s originating region (as outlined below):
United States Canada Australia United Kingdom Select Countries
Minimum Tilt Value $50 USD $50 CAD $50 AUD £30 €30

Note: Minimum Tilt value excludes contributions from Referred Friend and Tilt User.

The Tilt page created by the Referred Friend also must comply with the version of the Tilt Terms of Use applicable to that Referred Friend.

Payment

Any money earned under this Referral Program is sent via direct deposit to the bank account associated with either the Tilt user’s or referred friend’s Tilt profile within 10 business days of the qualifying Tilt’s expiration and the Tilt user providing valid payout information on their profile page.

Referral Links

You can use referral links only for personal and non-commercial purposes. Please share referral links with friends who will appreciate being invited to Tilt. Don’t share referral links with people you don’t know personally.

Multiple Referrals

A Tilt User referring a Referred Friend will only earn a Referral Reward if the Referred Friend creates a Tilt account for the first time with Tilt.

Limitations

No Referral Reward can be earned through the referral program as a result of fraudulent or illegal activities, or any other conduct that violates our terms of use or other policies. Neither Tilt Users nor Referred Friends can earn a Referral Reward through the Referral Program by creating multiple Tilt profiles.

Tilt reserves the right to investigate and review all activities related to the Referral Program and can cancel or modify referrals for any reason. If Tilt notices any activity that we believe is abusive, fraudulent, illegal, or violative of our Terms of Use or other policies, we may suspend profiles or restrict access to the referral program.
